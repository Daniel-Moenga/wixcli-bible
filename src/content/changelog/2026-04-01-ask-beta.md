The Ask AI beta is designed for a narrow job: help learners understand Wix CLI questions without
pretending to replace official documentation.

### Guardrails

- responses must cite supporting sources
- official Wix links are preferred when behavior is platform-sensitive
- weak evidence should narrow or refuse the answer

That design keeps the assistant helpful without letting it become a hallucination machine.
